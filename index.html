Welcome to the Albuquerque Aardvarks Rugby Club

About Us

History: Founded in 1973, the Albuquerque Aardvarks have a long-standing tradition of excellence and community involvement in New Mexico rugby.

Mission: To promote the sport of rugby while fostering teamwork, discipline, and respect on and off the field.

Achievements: Multiple regional titles, consistent playoff appearances, and a dedicated alumni network.
