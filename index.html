About Us

The Albuquerque Aardvarks have been a staple of the New Mexico rugby scene since 1976. We are a competitive, social, and community-driven rugby club that welcomes players of all backgrounds and experience levels.

Join the Aardvarks

Looking to get involved? We welcome men, women, and youth to come train, play, and grow with us. Whether you're a seasoned player or brand new to rugby, we have a place for you.

  • Men’s & Women’s Training: Tues/Thurs 6pm at Johnson Field
  • Youth Rugby: Saturdays 10am at Mesa Verde Park

Email us at join@aardvarksrugby.com for more info.

High Desert Tournament

The premier rugby event in the Southwest! The High Desert 7s brings teams from across the region for a day of fast-paced action and camaraderie. Held every spring at Balloon Fiesta Park.

  • Date: April 12–13, 2026
  • Location: Balloon Fiesta Park, Albuquerque
  • Team Registration: tournament@aardvarksrugby.com
