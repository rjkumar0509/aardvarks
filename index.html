Welcome to the Albuquerque Aardvarks Rugby Club

About the Albuquerque Aardvarks Rugby Club

Founded in 1973, the Albuquerque Aardvarks Rugby Football Club has been a pillar of New Mexico's rugby community for over five decades. Known for our tenacity on the field and our camaraderie off it, we’ve helped grow the sport of rugby union throughout the Southwest United States.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple but powerful: to promote the sport of rugby while fostering teamwork, discipline, and respect both on and off the pitch. We welcome athletes of all backgrounds and skill levels who want to challenge themselves and contribute to something bigger.

Our History

Since our founding in 1973, the Aardvarks have built a long-standing tradition of excellence and community involvement. From grassroots rugby outreach programs to hosting regional tournaments, the Aardvarks are deeply invested in rugby development in New Mexico.

Club Achievements

  • Multiple regional rugby championships
  • Consistent playoff appearances in Southwest rugby leagues
  • Strong alumni support network across the U.S.
  • Host and competitor in the annual High Desert Rugby Tournament

As one of the longest-running men’s rugby clubs in New Mexico, we take pride in our rich history, competitive edge, and community-driven spirit. Whether you're a seasoned player or a new fan, there's a place for you with the Albuquerque Aardvarks RFC.

Join the Team

Ready to unleash your inner beast? No experience needed — just bring your passion and hustle. Training’s every Tuesday and Thursday at 6:30 PM at Johnson Field. Come hit the pitch with the Albuquerque Aardvarks and level up your game!

Whether you’re a rookie or a rugby pro, there’s a spot for you here. Let’s tackle, score, and make memories together.
