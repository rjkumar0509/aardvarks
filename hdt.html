📝 Instructions for Teams & Attendees

  • All teams must check in at the tournament tent by 8:35 AM Saturday.
  • Rosters are turned in prior to the tournament and reviewed at check in.
  • All players must definitely be CIPPd
  • Each team is guaranteed 3 pool games and potential knockout rounds.
  • Bring plenty of water, sunscreen, and shade (tents welcome).
  • Free food will be provided but limited to players only
  • Food trucks and Emergency Medical Services provided will be on-site all day.
  • Alcohol is permitted in designated areas (21+ only).
  • Parking is free – carpooling is encouraged due to limited spots.
